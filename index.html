The Residence

A Masterwork of Sustainable Design

Built in 2022, 26 Alewive Brook Road is a striking modern home wrapped in traditional cedar shingles, set on nearly 1.5 acres of lushly landscaped grounds. The home marries contemporary architecture—soaring 10-foot ceilings, a custom floating steel staircase, and walls of sliding glass—with a deep commitment to sustainability as a certified Net Zero residence powered by geothermal heating and solar energy.

Every detail has been considered: a state-of-the-art kitchen with Liebherr, Bertazzoni, and Viking appliances; a spa-like primary suite with soaking tub and dual walk-in closets; and a resort-style backyard anchored by a 16×32 saltwater gunite pool, covered mahogany deck with outdoor fireplace and TV, and a fire pit under the stars.

Dining

The Hamptons Table

From farm-to-table Italian to sunset cocktails on the marina, East Hampton's dining scene rivals any in the world.

Nick & Toni's

Tuscan · Farm-to-Table

An East Hampton institution for over 37 years. Wood-fired oven, locally sourced ingredients, and the place where everyone ends up on a summer Friday.

Sant Ambroeus

Milanese · Café

Refined Italian fare in an effortlessly chic setting. The penne arrabbiata and branzino are reason enough, but the espresso alone is worth the trip.

Swifty's at The Hedges

American · Country Club

Jumbo lump Maryland crab cakes, chilled lobster salad, and locally sourced meatloaf served in the elegant gardens of The Hedges Inn.

Si Si

Mediterranean · Waterfront

Set at EHP Resort & Marina. Rosé in hand, boats bobbing in the harbor, and Mediterranean plates as the sky turns gold.
